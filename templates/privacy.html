Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we collect the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment or banking information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service to you. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and category breakdowns, and authenticating your account. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard practices to protect it from unauthorised access, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and hashed passwords. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting or database services) to operate the app, but these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data independently.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
